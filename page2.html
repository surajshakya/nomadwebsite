About Us

An apple orchard in the deodar forest, where the Himalayas begin.

Set in Kufri and surrounded by deodar forests with majestic views of the snow-capped Himalayas, Nomad is a place to slow down and make. Stay in traditional mud cottages, Swiss tents and basic tents; gather in our open-air Baithak and Café Lounge over local Himachali food, good coffee and organic drinks.

Stay With Us

Three ways to sleep under the deodars

Mud Cottages

Hand-built traditional cottages, warm and grounded, with murals and orchard views.

Swiss Tents

Comfortable canvas tents with proper beds — camp under the stars without roughing it.

Basic Tents

Simple, light-on-the-land shelter for travellers who want to wake with the mountains.

The Serai Residency · 02

Time, space and mountain light to go deep.

Our long-form residency gives makers room to work away from the noise — a studio, a community of artists, and the orchard outside the door. Open to artists across disciplines, year-round.
